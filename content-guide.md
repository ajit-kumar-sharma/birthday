## 🎵 Music Suggestions

The website includes background music. Replace the audio source with:

### Royalty-Free Options:
- **Bensound - "Little Idea"** *(currently used)*
- **Bensound - "Creative Minds"** - Uplifting, gentle
- **Mixkit - "Beautiful Dream"** - Piano, emotional
- **Pixabay - "Acoustic Breeze"** - Warm, intimate

### How to Change Music:
In `index.html`, find this line and replace the `src`:
```html
<source src="YOUR_AUDIO_URL_HERE" type="audio/mpeg">
```
